Encoder

A wrapper around encoders. Allows resetting encoders to a position.

Don't instantiate its subclasses directly. Instead, use their static creator methods

Parameters

encoderCPR

Counts per rotation of the encoder

unitPerRotation

Meters traveled per rotation of the motor

gearing

The factor the output changes by after being measured by the encoder (should be >= 1, not a reciprocal), e.g. this would be 70 if there were a 70:1 gearing between the encoder and the final output
